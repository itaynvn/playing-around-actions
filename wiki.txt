<p><i><b>Stigmella pyrivora</b></i> is a <a href="/wiki/Moth" title="Moth">moth</a> of the family <a href="/wiki/Nepticulidae" title="Nepticulidae">Nepticulidae</a>. It is endemic to <a href="/wiki/Cyprus" title="Cyprus">Cyprus</a>.
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:202px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Pyrus_syriaca_1.jpg" class="image"><img src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6f/Pyrus_syriaca_1.jpg/200px-Pyrus_syriaca_1.jpg" decoding="async" width="200" height="160" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6f/Pyrus_syriaca_1.jpg/300px-Pyrus_syriaca_1.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6f/Pyrus_syriaca_1.jpg/400px-Pyrus_syriaca_1.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3223" data-file-height="2579" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Pyrus_syriaca_1.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><i>Pyrus syriaca</i>, the host plant of the species</div></div></div>
<p>The larvae feed on <i><a href="/wiki/Pyrus_syriaca" title="Pyrus syriaca">Pyrus syriaca</a></i>. They <a href="/wiki/Mine_the_leaves" class="mw-redirect" title="Mine the leaves">mine the leaves</a> of their host plant. The mine resembles the mine of <i><a href="/wiki/Stigmella_paradoxa" title="Stigmella paradoxa">Stigmella paradoxa</a></i>.
</p>
