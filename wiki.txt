					<div id="mw-content-text" class="mw-body-content mw-content-ltr" lang="en" dir="ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>
<b>Tindivanam taluk</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Taluk" class="mw-redirect" title="Taluk">taluk</a> of <a href="/wiki/Viluppuram_district" title="Viluppuram district">Viluppuram district</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Indian_state" class="mw-redirect" title="Indian state">Indian state</a> of <a href="/wiki/Tamil_Nadu" title="Tamil Nadu">Tamil Nadu</a>. The headquarters of the taluk is the town of <a href="/wiki/Tindivanam" title="Tindivanam">Tindivanam</a>.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Demographics">Demographics</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Tindivanam_taluk&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Demographics">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2011, the taluk of Tindivanam had a population of 446,024 with 223,391 males and 222,633 females. There were 997 women for every 1,000 men. The taluk had a literacy rate of 67.66%. Child population in the age group below 6 years were 23,196 males and 22,445 females.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
